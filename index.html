Pokemon Yellow Version: Special Pikachu Edition

Game Information

Pokémon Yellow Version: Special Pikachu Edition, more commonly known as Pokémon Yellow, is a 1998 role-playing video game developed by Game Freak and published by Nintendo for the Game Boy. It is an enhanced version of Pokémon Red and Blue and is part of the first generation of the Pokémon video game series. It was first released in Japan on September 12, 1998, in Australia and North America in 1999 and in Europe in 2000. Pokémon Yellow is loosely based on the anime.
